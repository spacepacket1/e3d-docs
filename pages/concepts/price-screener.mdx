# Price Screener

The `/price` page is the dashboard of the system: price, volume, fragility, stories, and theses in one view.

## Why it is different

Most screeners show price and volume. E3D adds narrative intelligence:

- **Stories** — on-chain field notes
- **Theses** — structured conclusions
- **Fragility** — a stress gauge for how much you should trust the data
- **Trend picker** — a time lens that changes sparklines and narrative counts together

## Key ideas

- Stories and Theses are **interval-aware**: the counts change when you change the trend window
- Fragility tells you whether the setup looks sturdy or brittle
- Sorting is server-side across the full token universe

## What to watch first

1. Sort by Stories to find live activity
2. Sort by Theses to find fresh conviction
3. Look at Fragility before trusting the data too much
