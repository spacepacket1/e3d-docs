# Thesis Use Cases

Theses are most useful when they are part of a repeatable research workflow.

## Common workflows

### 1. Pre-trade review

Compare your own read with the agent’s structured thesis.

### 2. Signal sourcing

Use conviction sorting to find setups the agent thinks are strongest.

### 3. Risk monitoring

Track invalidation or confirmation as new stories arrive.

### 4. Weekly review

Evaluate which thesis patterns confirmed, expired, or were invalidated.

## How to interpret the feed

Theses are not trade instructions. They are structured hypotheses with explicit exit criteria, so the most important habit is to read the risk factors and verify the contributing stories.

## Practical example

If a token has multiple supporting stories and a high-conviction thesis, the next step is not immediate action — it is evidence review.

Ask:

- Do the supporting stories actually converge?
- Is the entry signal specific enough to monitor?
- What would invalidate the thesis?
- Is the entity a tradeable token or just an address?

## Why it matters

Theses help you turn a long stream of stories into a manageable set of decision-support objects.
